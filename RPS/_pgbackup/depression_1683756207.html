Comprehensive Depression Treatment

Depression is a prevalent mental health issue that affects millions of adults worldwide. At Real Psychiatric Services, we understand the impact of depressive disorders on individuals and their families. Our dedicated team of mental healthcare professionals offers comprehensive depression treatment options to help our clients on their journey to recovery. With a focus on effective medication management, telehealth services, and evidence-based therapies, we strive to be a valuable resource for those battling depression.

In Person and Tele Psychiatry

Real Psychiatric Services is conveniently located to serve our clients in the surrounding area. We understand that accessibility is crucial for those seeking mental healthcare, and our facility is designed to provide a welcoming and supportive environment. In addition to in-person visits, we also offer telehealth sessions for clients who prefer or require remote access to our services. This flexible approach ensures that our clients can receive the support they need, no matter their location.

Medication Management

For many adults with depressive disorders, medication management is a key component of their treatment plan. Our experienced psychiatrists work closely with clients to develop an individualized medication regimen that targets their specific symptoms and needs. By regularly monitoring and adjusting medications as needed, we ensure that our clients receive the most effective treatment possible.

Behavioral Health and Therapy

At Real Psychiatric Services, we recognize that a comprehensive approach to treating depression involves more than just medication. Our behavioral health team offers a range of evidence-based therapies designed to address the underlying issues contributing to a client's depressive disorder. Through individual and group therapy sessions, clients can explore and develop healthy coping strategies, improve their mood, and work towards lasting recovery.

Recovery and Support

Recovering from depression is an ongoing process that requires dedication and support. Our mental healthcare professionals are committed to providing the resources and guidance necessary for our clients to achieve and maintain their recovery goals. We work closely with clients to develop a tailored treatment plan that covers all aspects of their mental health needs, from medication management to therapy and beyond.

At Real Psychiatric Services, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and effective treatment options for adults with depressive disorders. By offering a range of services, including medication management, telehealth sessions, and behavioral health therapies, we aim to be a valuable resource for those seeking support and recovery. If you or a loved one is struggling with depression, we invite you to visit our location and get the help that you deserve. Schedule your appointment online for tele psychiatry or in person consultation in our Columbus, Ohio office
