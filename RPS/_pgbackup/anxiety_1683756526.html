Online therapy for anxiety

At Real Psychiatric Services, we understand that anxiety can have a significant impact on an individual's daily life and overall well-being. Serving the Columbus, Ohio area, our team of dedicated professionals offers both in-person and telepsychiatry services to help individuals manage and overcome the different types of anxiety disorders. Anxiety is a common mental health issue that can manifest in various forms, such as generalized anxiety disorder, social anxiety, panic disorder, and phobias. It is characterized by feelings of worry, fear, and unease that can interfere with one's daily activities and relationships.

A Comprehensive & Personalized Psychiatry Plan

At Real Psychiatric Services, our approach to treating anxiety involves a comprehensive and personalized plan tailored to each individual's unique needs. We believe in utilizing evidence-based techniques and therapies to help our clients better understand and manage their anxiety symptoms which include constant worry and can't-sleep. Our team of skilled mental health professionals will work closely with clients to identify the root causes of their anxiety and develop a customized treatment plan. This plan may include a combination of psychotherapy, such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), medication management, and lifestyle modifications.

Convenient & Accessable

We also recognize the importance of convenience and accessibility in mental health care, which is why we offer telepsychiatry services. This allows our clients to receive the support and treatment they need from the comfort of their own homes, making it easier for them to fit therapy into their busy schedules.

Our ultimate goal is to help individuals with anxiety regain control of their lives and achieve a sense of balance and well-being. If you or a loved one is struggling with anxiety, our compassionate team is here to provide the support and guidance you need on your journey towards better mental health.

In addition to therapy for anxiety, we offer treatment for other mental health disorders such as depression, ADHD, PTSD, and more.
