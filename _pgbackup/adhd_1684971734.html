Adult ADHD Treatment in Ohio

Evaluation and Treatment for Adult ADHD

Living with adult Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) can be challenging for everyday living, and it can also lead to anxiety, depression compound mental health challenges. Our team at Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner is dedicated to properly diagnosing and treating your condition with a personalized treatment plan.

Providing the Mental Health Services That are Right for You

Our mental health professionals in Columbus, Oh start off by providing an accurate and comprehensive assessment, taking the time to better understand your symptoms, lifestyle, concerns, and evaluate other mental health conditions that you may be experiencing such as anxiety or depression. This information is used to develop an individualized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Tele-Psychiatry Solutions: Online Treatment and Care via Our Advanced Technology Platform

We know that your life is busy and unpredictable; so we offer online treatment options via our Telehealth platform. We provide adult ADHD treatment Telehealth services to patients throughout Ohio, enabling convenient care from the comfort of your home; and in-person consultations in the greater Columbus area. With our supportive approach to therapy and counseling sessions you can finally feel empowered on your journey toward better mental health and overall well being.

Expert Mental Health Diagnosis & Medication Management

Our team of licensed psychiatric nurse practitioners is committed to helping you reach your full potential by providing a proper diagnosis via thorough evaluation. By utilizing evidence-based treatment options, including medication management, we maximize outcomes while minimizing side effects (assuming you follow instructions). Our goal is to provide the right medication for each patient while closely following up on their progress during periodic appointments.

Real Psychiatric Services: Supportive Counseling & Therapy

In addition to professional medical care from our providers, we’re proud to offer a range of therapeutic solutions tailored specifically for adults with ADHD at our locations in Columbus, Ohio. Our experienced therapists focus on addressing stress, PTSD, feeling stuck, lack of energy or motivation, and other challenges that may accompany ADHD. They’re dedicated to helping you create positive change in your life by addressing your mental health needs through a whole-person approach.

Our adult ADHD services are designed to empower you to overcome life’s challenges, giving you the tools and support necessary to live a happier and more fulfilling life.
