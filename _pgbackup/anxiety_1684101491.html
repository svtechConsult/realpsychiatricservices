Comprehensive Online Therapy for Anxiety in Columbus

At Real Psychiatric Services, we understand the impact anxiety can have on an individual’s daily life and overall well-being. Serving the Columbus, Ohio area, our team offers both in-person and telehealth services to help individuals manage and overcome different types of anxiety disorders.

Anxiety Disorders & Mental Health

Anxiety is a common mental health issue characterized by constant worry, fear, and unease. It can manifest in various forms such as:

- Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

- Social Anxiety

- Panic Disorder

- Phobias

These disorders can interfere with daily activities and relationships, making it essential to receive proper mental health care.

Telepsychiatry: Convenient & Accessible Mental Health Care

Recognizing the importance of convenience and accessibility, we provide telepsychiatry services (also known as telemedicine) which allow clients to receive support from their therapist via online therapy appointments. This approach enables individuals to get the help they need from the comfort of their own homes while fitting therapy into their busy schedules.

Telehealth Services Offered

Our telehealth services cover various aspects of mental health care including:

- Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

- Social Anxiety

- Panic Disorder

- Phobias

Personalized Psychiatry Plans with Evidence-Based Techniques

Our approach involves creating comprehensive and personalized psychiatry plans tailored to each individual’s unique needs. We utilize evidence-based techniques such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) along with medication management and lifestyle modifications when needed.

Skilled Therapists in Columbus Ready to Help

Our team of skilled therapists will work closely with clients to diagnose the root causes of their anxiety disorder and develop a customized plan to regain control over their lives. They provide consultation via online therapy or in-person sessions at our outpatient clinic in Columbus.
